QuikPix Media Converter

QuikPix is a web service application (Software As A Service) where users upload their raw photos and videos on and it renders and exports out different biotypes and resolutions to use based on different platforms' own specs (eg. high-quality gif, images, loop videos used on websites (personal/eCommerce), Shopify/Squarespace single product photos, Instagram stories, Instagram posts, Facebook posts, Twitter tweets, Pinterest, etc.). Users can then save it onto their device and share the image/video onto the specific platform.

Role: UI/UX Designer
Tools: Figma, JS React
Timeline: January - February 2020
